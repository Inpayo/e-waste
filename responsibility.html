RESPONSIBLE TECH

Adopting an ethical and sustainable approach to the lifecycle of our digital devices.

Pillars of Responsibility

1. Longevity

Maximize device life by taking good care of hardware and seeking repairs instead of replacements.

2. Conscious Habits

Avoid unnecessary marketing-driven upgrades and practice digital minimalism to save energy.

3. Ethical Support

Support manufacturers who prioritize sustainable sourcing and fair labor practices.

The Impact of Irresponsibility

Improper disposal leads to a "take-make-dispose" linear economy, causing toxic substances like lead and mercury to leach into soil and water.
