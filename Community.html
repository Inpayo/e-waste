What Can The Community Do?

1. PROMOTE DIGITAL LITERACY PROGRAMS

WHAT

Conduct workshops, school programs, and community talks on safe online behavior, privacy settings, cybersecurity, and critical thinking when using digital content.

BENEFITS

Reduces cases of online scams, cyberbullying, misinformation, and improves overall digital safety.

2. Encourage Parents & Youth Collaboration

WHAT

Create family-friendly tech guidelines or community campaigns that help parents guide children on screen time limits and responsible social media use.

BENEFITS

Builds healthier digital habits and strengthens trust between parents and children.

3. Create Safe Online Community Spaces

WHAT

Establish moderated community groups, forums, or channels where members can share information responsibly and respectfully.

BENEFITS

Prevents spread of harmful content, improves communication, and fosters a supportive environment.

4. Support Reporting & Awareness Campaigns

WHAT

Encourage community members to report cybercrimes, scams, and online harassment; run awareness campaigns about common online threats.

BENEFITS

Reduces online harm, increases awareness, and keeps community members safer.

5. Promote Tech for Good Initiatives

WHAT

Use technology for community improvement—such as apps for neighborhood safety, recycling, volunteer coordination, or health information.

BENEFITS

Strengthens community bonds, improves efficiency, and uses technology in positive, meaningful ways.

6. Encourage Ethical Sharing of Information

WHAT

Educate community members not to spread unverified news, fake information, or sensitive private content.

BENEFITS

Prevents misinformation, protects privacy, and maintains community trust

HOW CAN COMPANIES SUPPORT RESPONSIBLE TECH USAGE?

Be clear about data

which means telling people how their data is collected and usage of it.

Design fair systems

Check that AI and software do not make unfair choices.

Train employees

Teach workers about digital ethics and safe tech use.

Listen to feedback

Allow people to share their worries and fix problems quickly.

Look beyond the individual user and take into account technology’s potential impact and consequences on society.

Share how value is created in a transparent and understandable way.

Ensure best practice in technology that accounts for real, messy humans.

HOW FAMILIES CAN ENCOURAGE RESPONSIBLE TECH USE?

Responsible tech use for families involves balance, open communication and clear boundaries which help family members to connect with one another whilst limiting the tech usage

Discuss your family values in the context of technology use

Discuss with one another values when it comes to technology, how usage of technology affects or enhances those discussed values. For example, ask questions about what do we enjoy about technology or what are our biggest concerns.

Access your current technology use

Identify what reasons are family members using technology for, track screen time and reflect on patterns and concerns

Create a family media plan

Family media plan creates boundaries and expectations on how each family members should act when using technology. For example, device-free activities, time limits and screen-free zone.

Manage conflict around tech usage

Even with media plan, conflicts are always going to appear. Some of the solution on how to manage conflict about tech usage are encourage open dialogues, focus on solving one problem, connect with people using technology and be consistent in following the plan.

Recognise signs of stess caused by excessive tech use

Excessive use of technology will have an impact on both physically and mentally. Recognising the signs before it gets worse is crucial to ensure that health is always in good condition. Some of the signs are social withdrawal, changes in mood or behaviour, trouble sleeping.
