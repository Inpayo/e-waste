What Can The Community Do?

1. PROMOTE DIGITAL LITERACY PROGRAMS

WHAT

Conduct workshops, school programs, and community talks on safe online behavior, privacy settings, cybersecurity, and critical thinking when using digital content.

BENEFITS

Reduces cases of online scams, cyberbullying, misinformation, and improves overall digital safety.

2. Encourage Parents & Youth Collaboration

WHAT

Create family-friendly tech guidelines or community campaigns that help parents guide children on screen time limits and responsible social media use.

BENEFITS

Builds healthier digital habits and strengthens trust between parents and children.

3. Create Safe Online Community Spaces

WHAT

Establish moderated community groups, forums, or channels where members can share information responsibly and respectfully.

BENEFITS

Prevents spread of harmful content, improves communication, and fosters a supportive environment.

4. Support Reporting & Awareness Campaigns

WHAT

Encourage community members to report cybercrimes, scams, and online harassment; run awareness campaigns about common online threats.

BENEFITS

Reduces online harm, increases awareness, and keeps community members safer.

5. Promote Tech for Good Initiatives

WHAT

Use technology for community improvement—such as apps for neighborhood safety, recycling, volunteer coordination, or health information.

BENEFITS

Strengthens community bonds, improves efficiency, and uses technology in positive, meaningful ways.

6. Encourage Ethical Sharing of Information

WHAT

Educate community members not to spread unverified news, fake information, or sensitive private content.

BENEFITS

Prevents misinformation, protects privacy, and maintains community trust
